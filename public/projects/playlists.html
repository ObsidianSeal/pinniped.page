Playlists

All of the YouTube Music playlists I’ve created, in one convenient place. I use this page to share music with friends and family, to avoid using a song in more than one monthly playlist, to investigate my playlist data, and to remind myself of music I may have forgotten. Enjoy!

Listed below each playlist’s title is the playlist’s number of tracks, length, and the date at which it was last updated on this page. Click on a playlist’s “listen” button to open it in YouTube Music or “rate” button to rate all the tracks in the playlist out of five stars! Click on the arrow to the left of a playlist’s title to view its list of tracks, including titles, artists, albums, and release years. As for everything else, you can hover over buttons and links to see what they do or where they lead.

There are some new statistics in the ‘Data’ section! Go check them out!
