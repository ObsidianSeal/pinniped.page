Gameplay Notes

  • Red = light pieces (goes first)
  • Blue = dark pieces
  • Click and drag to move pieces
  • If a move is illegal, the piece is put back
  • For promotion: the square is divided in four, click on the piece you want
  • For castling: drag the king onto the target rook
  • When the game is over (checkmate or draw), hold space to see the board
  • Then, press escape or enter to quit

Other Notes

  • You might need to update your Java to run this program, see the link at the top of the page
  • The window's title shows the turn
  • The title also shows the values of the pieces captured
