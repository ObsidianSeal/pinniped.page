How to Play - THE BASICS

  • Drag items from the sidebar (or “discoveries container”) into the main area (or “workspace”) to work with them
  • Drag an item onto another in the workspace to see if they combine
  • Discovered items show up in the sidebar
  • Click the trash icon in the bottom right of the workspace to clear it
  • Your progress (number of discoveries) is shown in the bottom left
  • You can search for specific discovered items with the search box
  • Next to the search box are the sorting options: you can sort by time (most recent discoveries last), name (a-z), and colour
  • As you discover new things, your progress is saved to the URL
  • You can go back to the same URL (from your browser history or saved location) later to reload your progress

How to Play - TIPS & TRICKS

  • The search box is case sensitive
  • You can search with RegEx; example searches:  ^[Mm]   \d   ^e.*e$ 
  • Left or middle click items in the sidebar to spawn them somewhere in the main area
  • Middle or double click items in the main area to duplicate them
  • Drag items into the sidebar to remove them
  • You can also remove items from the main area by right clicking them
  • Don’t forget to try to combine two of the same item!
  • If you’re stuck, the animation below shows how to quickly get otter (and a bunch of other things)

NEW: Book of Items

  • Click the “discovered fraction” in the bottom left to access version 1.6.0’s new feature: the Book of Items

Book of Items

  • Each item is numbered
  • Item icons, names, and colours are shown
  • The names of items that have not yet been discovered are obfuscated with question marks
  • The number to the left of the arrow is how many unique recipes make the item
  • The number to the right of the arrow is how many unique recipes this item is part of
  • Basically, the Book of Items is a way to get hints and learn more about the items
  • Exit the Book of Items by clicking the close button, pressing ‘ESCAPE’, or clicking outside the box
