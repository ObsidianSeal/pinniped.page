Introducing: Blog Archive

I think 10 blog posts on the Blog page is enough, so, now that there are more than 10, I have archived the oldest ones. What does that mean? Well, they’re just accessible from another page: the Blog Archive (now Blog+) page (pinniped.page/blog+). That page lists the blog posts in a very similar way to the main blog page, but they take up much less space on the page. The links to the posts have not changed, the posts’ contents have not changed, they’re just not listed on the main blog page. Every time a post is posted, the oldest one will be archived so there’s always 10 on the blog page.
